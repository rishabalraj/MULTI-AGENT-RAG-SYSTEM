COMPLETE EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK 2024 - TechCorp Solutions

CHAPTER 1 COMPANY OVERVIEW
TechCorp Solutions was founded in 2010 with a vision to deliver world class software solutions to enterprises globally. We have grown from a 10 person startup to a 5000 employee organization with offices in Bangalore Mumbai Delhi Hyderabad and Chennai. Our mission is to empower businesses through innovative technology solutions that drive growth efficiency and digital transformation. Our values are Innovation Integrity Excellence and Collaboration.

CHAPTER 2 ONBOARDING PROCESS
Before your first day you must submit all original documents for verification including Aadhar card PAN card passport size photographs educational certificates and previous employment documents. Complete the online joining form on the HR portal. Sign the offer letter employment contract and NDA agreement digitally. Complete the background verification process which typically takes 5 to 7 business days. On your first day arrive at office at 9am and meet HR representative. Complete physical documentation and biometric registration by 9:30am. Orientation session begins with HR team at 10am. Meet your reporting manager and team members at 11am. Lunch with your assigned buddy at 12pm. IT setup including laptop email and system access at 1pm. Introduction to company tools and platforms at 3pm. Office tour and introduction to other departments at 4pm. First day wrap up with HR at 5pm. First week schedule is Day 1 HR orientation paperwork and IT setup. Day 2 department overview and role specific briefing. Day 3 product training and technology stack introduction. Day 4 security compliance and data protection training. Day 5 meet senior leadership and company culture session. Every new employee is assigned an experienced buddy from their team for the first 3 months. New employees get access to Slack Jira Confluence GitHub and Zoom. Complete all 12 mandatory training modules on the LMS portal within 30 days.

CHAPTER 3 COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS
Your CTC is divided into basic salary which is 40 percent of CTC. House Rent Allowance is 20 percent of basic salary. Variable pay ranges from 10 to 30 percent of annual CTC. Salaries are credited on the last working day of each month. Salary slips are available on the employee self service portal. TechCorp provides comprehensive group health insurance to all permanent employees from day one of joining. The policy covers the employee spouse and up to 2 dependent children. Sum insured is 5 lakhs per family per year. Coverage includes hospitalization outpatient treatment dental care vision care and maternity. Cashless treatment is available at 5000 plus network hospitals. Premium is fully paid by the company for employee and family. Company contributes 12 percent of basic salary to EPF account. Employee also contributes 12 percent of basic salary to EPF. EPF statements are available on the EPFO member portal. Employees are eligible for gratuity after completing 5 years. Gratuity is calculated as 15 days salary for each year of service. Annual performance bonus is disbursed every April. Rating 5 Outstanding gets 30 percent of annual CTC as bonus. Rating 4 Exceeds Expectations gets 20 percent. Rating 3 Meets Expectations gets 10 percent. Rating 2 Below Expectations gets no bonus.

CHAPTER 4 LEAVE POLICY
Annual Leave is 20 days per calendar year credited on January 1st. Sick Leave is 12 days per year for medical related absences. Casual Leave is 6 days per year for personal emergencies. Maternity Leave is 26 weeks fully paid for female employees. Paternity Leave is 15 days paid leave for new fathers. Bereavement Leave is 5 days for immediate family member loss. Marriage Leave is 5 days one time leave for own marriage. Study Leave is up to 10 days per year for exams with approval. All leaves except emergency sick leaves must be applied minimum 2 days in advance on the HR portal. Manager approval is mandatory for all leaves. Leaves without approval will be marked as Loss of Pay. Carry forward limit for annual leave is 10 days per year.

CHAPTER 5 REMOTE WORK POLICY
All permanent employees who have completed 90 day probation are eligible for remote work. Employees on performance improvement plan are not eligible for remote work. Maximum 3 days work from home per week is allowed. Remaining minimum 2 days must be worked from office per week. Core hours of 10am to 3pm must be observed even while remote. All scheduled meetings must be attended via video with camera on. Response time on Slack and email must be within 30 minutes during core hours. Annual equipment allowance of Rs 15000 for remote work setup. Internet reimbursement of Rs 1000 per month for WFH days. Minimum internet speed of 25 Mbps required for video calls.

CHAPTER 6 PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year. Mid year review happens in July for January to June period. Annual review happens in January for July to December period. Rating 5 is Outstanding which means significantly exceeded all goals. Rating 4 is Exceeds Expectations which means exceeded most goals. Rating 3 is Meets Expectations which means achieved all goals. Rating 2 is Below Expectations which means partially achieved goals. Rating 1 is Unsatisfactory which means failed to meet most goals. Employees rated 1 or 2 consecutively will be placed on Performance Improvement Plan. PIP duration is 90 days with clear measurable improvement goals. Weekly check-ins with manager and HR during PIP period.

CHAPTER 7 CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees must maintain highest standards of professionalism. Treat all colleagues clients and visitors with respect and dignity. Harassment bullying discrimination of any kind is strictly prohibited. Violation of harassment policy results in immediate termination. All company information is strictly confidential. Do not share client data project details or financial information. Get approval from marketing team before posting about company on social media.

CHAPTER 8 IT AND SECURITY POLICY
Company issued laptops must be used for all official work. Do not install unauthorized software on company devices. Lock your screen whenever you leave your desk unattended. Report lost or stolen devices to IT immediately. Use strong passwords with minimum 12 characters. Passwords must include uppercase lowercase numbers and symbols. Change passwords every 90 days as prompted by the system. Never share passwords with anyone including IT team members. Use the company VPN when working from outside office network. Store all work files on company approved cloud storage only. Complete mandatory cybersecurity training every 6 months.

CHAPTER 9 GRIEVANCE AND ESCALATION
Step 1 discuss the issue directly with your immediate manager. Step 2 if unresolved escalate to skip level manager within 7 days. Step 3 raise formal grievance ticket on HR portal if still unresolved. Step 4 HR will investigate and respond within 15 working days. Step 5 final escalation to HR head if not satisfied with resolution. TechCorp has zero tolerance for sexual harassment at workplace. Internal Complaints Committee is constituted as per POSH Act. Any employee can report harassment to ICC confidentially. Investigation will be completed within 90 days of complaint.

CHAPTER 10 EXIT PROCESS
Submit resignation letter to manager and HR via email. Notice period is 2 months for all permanent employees. Notice period buyout is allowed with manager and HR approval. Return all company assets including laptop access card and ID. Complete knowledge transfer document for your replacement. Attend exit interview with HR team on last working day. Full and final settlement is processed within 45 days of exit. Experience letter and relieving letter issued within 7 days of exit.
